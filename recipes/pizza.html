SWEET & SOUR CHICKEN PIZZA WITH BBQ SAUCE

Sweet & Sour Chicken Pizza combines the richness of barbecue with the comfort of pizza which makes it a show-stealer on the dining table

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough (Homemade or Store-Bought).
  • Chicken Breasts.
  • Sweet & Sour BBQ Sauce.
  • Red Onion.
  • Pineapple.
  • Spring Onions.

Recipe

  1. First, grill the chicken and season as you like. Preheat the oven to 200C.
  2. Place the pizza dough on a 26cm lightly greased baking pan or pizza tray.
  3. Spread with two tablespoons of the BBQ sauce and top with cheese, chicken, onion, and pineapple.
  4. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until cooked through.
  5. Sprinkle the spring onions on top and drizzle with the remaining sauce
  6. Serve immediately.
