Chocolate Milkshake Dessert

Chocolate Milkshake with Ice Cream is Yummy. Here's how you make the dessert.

Ingredients

  • 500ml of milk (Semi skimmed preferable)
  • 4 large scoops of chocolate ice cream
  • 10 marshmallows
  • 60 ml of double whipped cream
  • 1/4 cup of chocolate syrup
  • Sweet popcorn
  • 2 cookies
  • 2 waffles
  • Optional: 2 table spoons of hazelnuts

Recipe

  1. Start by getting a small tablespoon and spreading chocolate along the inside of the glass
  2. Allow the ice-cream to become softer at room temp.
  3. For optimal results place the glass into the freezer to let it cool.
  4. Place the 500ml of milk into a blender along with the chocolate ice cream scoops
  5. Let the blender do its magic and once smooth pour the mix into the mason jar glasses
  6. Add the marshmallows, whipped cream, sweet popcorn, waffles and cookies.
  7. Decorate the treat with a warm drizzle of chocolate spread.Optionally add hazlenuts at this point
  8. Serve right away with a straw
